Our Premium Process

From the shaded tea fields of Uji, Kyoto to your cup. Discover how we maintain the premium quality of our traditional matcha.

1. 100% Shaded Grown

The tea leaves are shaded from sunlight for 3-4 weeks before harvest to boost chlorophyll and amino acids.

2. Hand-Picked

Only the youngest, most vibrant green top leaves are carefully selected by hand during the spring harvest.

3. Stone-Ground

Leaves are slowly ground using traditional granite stone mills to preserve flavor, color, and rich nutrients.
